📋 Call Format Reference

MAYDAY — Distress
1.MAYDAY MAYDAY MAYDAY
2.Station called
3.Callsign × 3
4.Nature of emergency
5.Intentions
6.Position
7.Altitude / Level
8.Souls on board
9.Fuel remaining
10.Any other info
PAN-PAN — Urgency
1.PAN-PAN PAN-PAN PAN-PAN
2.Station called
3.Callsign × 3
4.Nature of urgency
5.Intentions
6.Position
7.Altitude / Level
8.Souls on board
9.Fuel remaining
Key Difference
MAYDAY = life in immediate danger. PAN-PAN = urgent situation that may become dangerous. When in doubt between the two — always declare MAYDAY. You can always downgrade, never too late to upgrade.
